Knocking out SOBIR1 in <i>Nicotiana benthamiana</i> by CRISPR/Cas9, abolishes the functionality of the transgenic receptor-like protein Cf-4, recognizing the Avr4 effector of the fungus <i>Cladosporium fulvum</i>.
