<i>Elsinoë ampelina</i> is an ascomycetous fungus that causes grape anthracnose, a potentially devastating disease worldwide. Here, we report a 28.29 Mb high-quality genome sequence of <i>E. ampelina</i> YL-1 that encodes 8,057 predicted protein-coding genes and represents the first sequenced genome assembly of <i>E. ampelina</i>. This study adds to the current genomic resources for the genus <i>Elsinoë</i> and paves the way for research on comparative genomic studies, <i>E. ampelina</i>-grape interactions, and improvement of management strategies.
