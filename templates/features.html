Composition of PlantConnectome


PlantConnectome was contructed by surveying numerous sources, shown in the pie chart to the right, with GPT.

Unifying more than 100,000 plant studies, PlantConnectome can be used to study genes, metabolites, tissues and other biological entities, illuminating over 350,000 relationships altogether.

Searching PlantConnectome

PlantConnectome offers 5 search options:


Term Searches

Search for entities using a specific keyword or phrase with this option. Users can also search for aliases, exact matches, sub-strings, and non-alphanumeric results.

Author Search

Search for entities using author names.

Pubmed ID Search
